3 Knitting

Here is the chapter where I teach you how to knit! It mainly involves visiting lots of yarn stores and filling your home with squashy yarns of varying types and lots of different kinds of needles. Did you know there are lots of knitting needles? There are: - straight needles to knit flat objects like scarves - circular needles to knit in the round for things like sweaters and hats You can also use circular needles to knit flat and multiple straight needles to knit circular because knitters are creative problem solvers! There are two basic stitches: 1. knit 2. purl There are also fancy ways you can combine these stitches, and add in flair like yarn-overs, cables, and drops.
